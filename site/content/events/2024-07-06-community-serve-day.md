Once a year, our Arbor family comes together to serve our community in a tangible way, and we can't wait to do it again! This year, you'll have the opportunity to serve either at a local school or right here at Arbor with a variety of on-campus projects. Whether you're passionate about serving in the community or helping care for our church home, there's a place for everyone. After we've wrapped up our projects, we'll gather back at Arbor for a BBQ lunch! A chance to relax, share stories from the day, and celebrate all that we accomplished together. We hope you'll stick around for great food and even better company! To sign up, visit **communityserveday.org** and register for **Woodin Elementary**.
