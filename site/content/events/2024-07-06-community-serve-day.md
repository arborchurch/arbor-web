Once a year, our Arbor family comes together to serve our community in a tangible way, and we can't wait to do it again! This year, you'll have the opportunity to serve either at a local school or right here at Arbor with a variety of on-campus projects. Whether you're passionate about serving in the community or helping care for our church home, there's a place for everyone. After we've wrapped up our projects, we'll gather back at Arbor for a BBQ lunch! A chance to relax, share stories from the day, and celebrate all that we accomplished together. We hope you'll stick around for great food and even better company! **IMPORTANT** : To sign up for Woodin Elementary please do that at  **[communityserveday.org](http://communityserveday.org).** If you are planning to serve at Arbor church that morning please register with the link below. Additionally, anyone who is coming back/staying for the lunch BBQ please register you and your family (also in link below)
