Join us during the **10:30 service on Nov 23rd** for Discover Arbor! This is a gathering where you can learn about Arbor's history, mission, vision, and values. You'll also have the opportunity to ask questions and meet others who are new to our community. Everyone is welcome, whether you're new to the church or just interested in learning more. Don't miss this chance to discover next steps for getting more involved in the life of our church.

If you have children, make sure to check them into kid classrooms before meeting us in the Arbor Offices for our time together!
