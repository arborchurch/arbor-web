> God is a righteous judge, a God who displays his wrath every day.

-- Psalm 7:11

Wrath is unique among the seven deadly sins because it is the only one that... isn't a necessarily a sin. God gets angry too! This week, from the story of Job, Ryan illustrates the difference between righteous anger and sinful wrath.
